hazelwort is said to be beneficial for liver complaints, an ounce being taken in a hemina of diluted honey wine. it purges the bowels after the manner of hellebore, and is good for dropsy, the hypochondria, the uterus and for jaundice. when added to must it makes a diuretic wine. it is dug up when the leaves are forming; it is dried and then stored up. in the shade it very quickly goes mouldy.
