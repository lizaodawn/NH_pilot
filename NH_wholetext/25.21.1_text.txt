melampus is well known for his skill in the arts of divination. from him one kind of hellebore is called melampodion. some hold that the discovery is due to a shepherd called melampus, who noticed that his she-goats were purged after browsing upon the plant, and by administering the milk of these goats cured the daughters of proetus of their madness. wherefore it is well to give here together an account of every kind of hellebore.
