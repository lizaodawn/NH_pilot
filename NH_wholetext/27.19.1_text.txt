aster is called by some bubonion, because it is a sovereign remedy for affections of the groin. its stem has two or three oblong leaves, and on the top are little heads with rays like stars. in drink it is also taken for snake bites. but as medicine for the groin it is enjoined to be plucked with the left hand, and to be tied as an amulet next the girdle. as an amulet it is also good for sciatica.
