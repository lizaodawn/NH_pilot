of vultures the black are the strongest. no one has ever reached their nests, and consequently there have actually been persons who have thought that they fly here from the opposite side of the globe. this is a mistake: they make their nests on extremely lofty crags. their chicks indeed are often seen, usually in pairs. the most learned augur of our age, umbricius, states that they lay thirteen eggs, but use one of them for cleaning the remaining eggs and the nest and then throw it away; but that three days before they lay the eggs they fly to some place where there will be dead bodies.
