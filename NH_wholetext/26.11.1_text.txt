for the treatment of quinsy argemonia is taken in wine, hyssop is boiled down with figs and used as a gargle, peucedanum is used with rennet of the seal in equal parts, and proserpinaca pounded with sprats-brine and oil, or else held beneath the tongue. cinquefoil juice also, in doses of three cyathi. this also used as gargle is good for all affections of the throat; verbascum taken in water is specific for the tonsils.
