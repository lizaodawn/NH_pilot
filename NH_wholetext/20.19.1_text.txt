elecampane too chewed by people fasting strengthens the teeth. if it is taken from the ground so as not to touch it, a confection of it is healing for a cough; the juice moreover of the boiled root expels worms, and dried in the shade its powdered form cures cough, convulsions, flatulence and affections of the trachea. it keeps off the bite of poisonous creatures. an application of the leaves steeped in wine is used for lumbago.
