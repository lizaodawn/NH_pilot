the first of the artificial wines, which is called weak wine, is made from real wine in the following manner: ten quarts of white must and half that quantity of water are kept boiling till a considerable amount of the water is boiled away. other people put in five quarts of seawater and the same amount of rainwater and leave the mixture in the sun for 40 days to evaporate. this drink is given to invalids for whom it is feared that wine may be harmful.
