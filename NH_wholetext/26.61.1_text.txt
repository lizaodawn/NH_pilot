nymphaea heraclia, as i have said, takes away altogether sexual desire; a single draught of it does so for forty days; sexual dreams too are prevented if it is taken in drink on an empty stomach and eaten with food. applied to the genitals the root also cheeks not only desire but also excessive accumulation of semen. for this reason it is said to make flesh and to improve the voice. sexual desire is excited by the upper part of xiphium root given in wine as a draught; also by the plant called eremuos agrios and by ormenos agrios crushed with pearl barley.
