the 'nasamonitis,' or 'stone of the nasamones,' is blood-red with black veins. the 'nebritis,' or 'fawn stone,' which is sacred to father liber, derives its name from its resemblance to a fawnskin, but there is another stone of the same kind that is black and white. 'nipparene,' which gets its name from a city and tribe of persia, is like the tooth of a hippopotamus.
