there have been various and numerous cases of eminence in the other kinds of excellence. cato the first of that name in the gens porcia is deemed to have exemplified the three supreme human achievements, excelling alike as orator, as general and as senator; all of which distinctions seem to me to have been achieved though not previously yet with greater brilliance in the case of scipio aemilianus, and that moreover without the very wide unpopularity that handicapped cato. so it may be counted an exceptional fact about cato that he took part in forty-four actions at law and was sued more frequently than anybody else and always acquitted.
