for dropsy is given in drink wool grease in wine mixed with a little myrrh, in doses the size of a hazel nut. some also add goose grease in myrtle-wine. the dirt from the udders of sheep has the same effect, as has the dried flesh of a hedgehog taken by the mouth. an application too of dogs vomit to the abdomen brings away, we are assured, the dropsical fluid.
