an application of moss that has grown in water is good for gout, and mixed with oil for painful and swollen ankles. rubbing with foam of water removes warts, as does also sand of the sea shores, especially fine sand whitened by the sun; it is used in medicine as a covering for drying the bodies of patients suffering from dropsy or catarrhs. so much for waters; now for the products of water. i shall begin, as elsewhere, with the chief of them, that is, with salts and sponge.
