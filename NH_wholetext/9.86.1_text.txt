besides these eases i observe that authors renowned for their wisdom express surprise at there being a star in the sea: that is the shape of the fish which has rather little, flesh inside it but a rather hard rind outside. they say that this fish contains such fiery heat that it scorches all the things it touches in the sea, and digests all food immediately. i cannot readily say by what experiments this has been ascertained, and i should consider a fact that there is daily opportunity of experiencing to be much more worth recording.
