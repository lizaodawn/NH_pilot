related to wine is sapa, which is must boiled down until one third remains. that made from white must is the better. it is used as an antidote to cantharides, buprestis, pine caterpillars, which are called pityocampae, salamanders, and to all poisonous bites. taken in drink with onions it brings away the afterbirth and also the dead foetus. fabianus states that it is poisonous if a man drinks it fasting just after a bath.
