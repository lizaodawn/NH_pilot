privet, if it is the same tree as the cypros of the east, has its own uses in europe. its juice benefits sinews, joints and chills; its leaves everywhere are used to treat chronic ulcer and, with a sprinkling of salt, sores in the mouth; the berries are employed for phthiriasis, and the berries or the leaves for chafing between the thighs. the berries also cure the pip in chickens.
