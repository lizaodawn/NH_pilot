for snake bites efficacious remedies are considered to be fresh dung of sheep boiled down in wine and applied, and mice cut in two and placed on the wound. the nature of mice is not to be despised, especially in their agreement, as i have said, with the heavenly bodies, for the number of their liver filaments becomes greater or less with the light of the moon. the magi declare that if a mouse's liver in a fig is offered to pigs, that animal will follow the offerer, adding that it has a similar effect on a human being also, but that the spell is broken by drinking a cyathus of oil.
