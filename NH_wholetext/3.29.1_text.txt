moesia: joining up to pannonia is the province called moesia, which runs, with the course of the danube, as far as the euxine. it commences at the confluence previously mentioned. in it are the dardani, the celegeri, the triballi, the timachi, the moesi, the thracians, and the scythians who border on the euxine. the more famous among its rivers are the margis, which rises in the territory of the dardani, the pingus, the timachus, the oescus which rises in mount rhodope, and, rising in mount haemus, the utus, the asamus, and the ieterus.
