the myrris, also called myrriza or myrra, is very like hemlock in stem, leaves and flower, but smaller and more slender, and not unpleasant as a food. with wine it promotes menstruation and facilitates delivery. it is said that it is also healthful to take it in drink in time of plague. given in broth it helps consumptives. it sharpens the appetite and allays the bite of poisonous spiders. sores too on the face or head are cured by its juice obtained by steeping the plant in water for three days.
