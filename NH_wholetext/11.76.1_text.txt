it is said that the filaments in the tiny livers of mice correspond with the number of the days of the moon in the month, and are found to correspond with its degree of light; and also that they grow larger with winter. rabbits are often found in southern spain with a double set of internal organs. one of the two filaments of toads ants do not touch, because of their poison, as is believed. the liver is extremely capable of enduring age, and has been proved by instances of sieges to last a hundred years.
