paliurus too is a species of thorn. its seed the africans call zura; it is very efficacious for scorpion sting, and likewise for stone and cough. the leaves have an astringent quality. the root disperses superficial abscesses, gatherings and boils; taken in drink it is diuretic. a decoction of it in wine checks looseness of the bowels and neutralizes the poison of serpents. the root especially is given in wine.
