moreover cato also says that there are certain crops which themselves nourish the land: 'cornland is manured by grain, lupine, beans and vetches'; just as on the contrary: 'chick-pea, because it is pulled up by the roots and because it is salt, barley, fenugreek, bitter vetch, - these all scorch up a cornland, as do all plants that are pulled up by the roots. do not plant stone-fruit in cornland.' - virgil holds the opinion that cornland is also scorched by flax, oats and poppies.
