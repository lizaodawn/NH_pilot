flowers as a distinction have been given by the roman people only to a scipio. he was surnamed serapio because of his likeness to a pig-dealer of that name. he died in his tribunate, being high in the esteem of the common people and worthy of the family of the africani, but not leaving enough. estate to pay for his funeral. so the people contracted for his funeral, contributing their pence, and scattered flowers from every point of vantage along all the route.
