dittander (pepperwort) is considered to be one of the caustic plants. so it clears the complexion, but produces sores on the skin, which, however, are easily cured with wax and rose oil. thus used, it always removes leprous sores and psoriasis easily, as well as the sores left by scars. it is said that in cases of toothache, if it be attached to the arm on the side where the pain is, this is diverted to it.
