the swallow-fish flies just exactly like a bird, and so does the kite-fish. the fish on this account called the lamp-fish rises to the surface of the sea, and on calm nights gives a light with its fiery tongue which it puts out from its mouth. the fish that has got its name from its horns raises these up about 18 inches out of the sea. the sea-snake, again, when caught and placed on the sand, with marvellous rapidity digs itself a hole with its beak.
