in this department of nature also there are cases of augury; even fish have foreknowledge of events. during the sicilian war when augustus was walking on the shore a fish leapt out of the sea at his feet, a sign which the priests interpreted as meaning that although sextus pompeius was then adopting neptune as his father - so glorious were his naval exploits - yet those who at that time held the seas would later be beneath the feet of caesar.
