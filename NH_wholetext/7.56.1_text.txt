before we quit the subject of man's nature it seems suitable to point out the various discoveries of different persons. father liber instituted buying and selling, and also invented the emblem of royalty, the crown, and the triumphal procession. ceres discovered corn, men having hitherto lived on acorns; she also invented grinding corn and making flour in attica (or, as others say, in sicily), and for this was deemed a goddess. also she first gave laws, though others have thought this was done by rhadamanthus.
