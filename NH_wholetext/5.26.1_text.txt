pamphylia: the former name of pamphylia was mopsopia. the pamphylian sea joins up to that of cilicia. the towns of pamphylia are side, aspendus, situate on the side of a mountain, pletenissum, and perga. there is also the promontory of leucolla, the mountain of sardemisus, and the rivers eurymedon, which flows past aspendus, and catarractes, near to which is lyrnesus: also the towns of olbia, and phaselis, the last on this coast.
