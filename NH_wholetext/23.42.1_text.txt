almond oil cleanses, makes the body supple, smoothes the skin, improves the complexion, and with honey removes spots on the face. a decoction also with rose oil or honey and pomegranate rind is good for the ears, kills the little worms in them, and dears away hardness of hearing, vague noises and singing, incidentally relieving headache and pains in the eves. combined with wax it cures boils and sunburn. with wine it cleans away running sores and scaly eruptions; with melilot, eondylomata. applied to the head moreover by itself it induces sleep.
