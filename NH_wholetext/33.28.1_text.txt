gold-solder is also used in medicine, mixed with wax and olive oil, for cleansing wounds; likewise applied dry by itself it dries wounds and draws them together. it is also given in cases of quinsy or asthma, to be taken as an electuary with honey. it acts as an emetic, and also is used as an ingredient in salves for sores in the eyes and in green plasters for relieving pains, and drawing together scars. this kind of gold-solder is called by medical men remedial solder, and is not the same as orobitis.
