lime is commonly used also in pharmacy, preferably when freshly calcined and unslaked. it has caustic, dispersive and drawing effects, and checks an onset of ulcers which shows signs of spreading quickly. it brings about the formation of scars when it is mixed as a liniment with vinegar and rose oil and is later blended with wax and rose oil. it is a cure also for dislocations when applied with liquid resin or pork fat mixed with honey, and the same mixture, moreover, cures scrofulous sores.
