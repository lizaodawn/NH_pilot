also we must not leave out a plant that at babylon is grown on thorn-bushes, because it will not live anywhere else — just as mistletoe grows on trees, but the plant in question will only grow on what is called the 'royal thorn.' it is a remarkable fact that it buds on the same day as it has been planted — this is done just at the rising of the dog-star — and it very quickly takes possession of the whole of the tree. it is used in making spiced wine, and is cultivated for that purpose. this thorn also grows on the long walls at athens.
