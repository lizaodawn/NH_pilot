thelyphonon is a plant called scorpion some because its root has the shape of one. a mere touch of it kills scorpions, and so it is taken in drink for their stings. it is said that a dead scorpion, if smeared with white hellebore, comes to life again. thelyphonon kills every kind of quadruped if its root be applied to the genitals, the leaf indeed, which is like that of cyclamen, does so before the end of the same day. the plant itself is jointed, and grows in shaded places. good for scorpion bite is the juice of betony ore plantain.
