fennel has been made famous, as we have said, by serpents, which taste it to east off their old skin and with its juice improve their eyesight. consequently it has been inferred that by fennel juice especially can dimness of human vision also be removed. this juice is collected when the stem is swelling to bud, dried in the sun and applied in honey as an ointment. the most esteemed is gathered in spain from the teardrops of the plant. it is also made from fresh seed and from incisions in the root when germination has first begun.
