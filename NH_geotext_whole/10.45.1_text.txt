the wheatear indeed actually has fixed days of retirement: it goes into hiding at the rising of the dog-star and comes out after its setting, doing both on the actual days, which is surprising. also the golden oriole, which is yellow all over, is not seen in winter but comes out about midsummer. blackbirds are born white at cyllene in arcadia, but nowhere else. the ibis is black only in the neighbourhood of pelusium, being white in all other places.
