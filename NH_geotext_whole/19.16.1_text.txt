there is another kind of laser-wort called magydaris, which is gentler and less violent in its effects, and has no juice; this grows in the neighbourhood of syria, not being found in the cyrenaica region. also there is a plant growing in great abundance on mount parnassus that is called laser-wort plant by some persons. all these varieties are used for adulteration, bringing discredit on a very salutary and useful commodity. the first test of the genuine article is in the colour, which is reddish, and white inside when the mass is broken; and the next test is if the juice that drips out is transparent and melts very quickly in saliva. it is employed as an ingredient in a great many medicaments.
