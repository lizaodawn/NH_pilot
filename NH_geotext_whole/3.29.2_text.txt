the breadth of illyricum at its widest part is 325 miles, and its length from the river arsia to the river drinius 530; from the drinius to the promontory of acroceraunia agrippa states to be 175 miles, and he says that the entire circuit of the italian and illyrian gulf is 1700 miles. in this gulf, according to the limits which we have drawn, are two seas, the ionian in the first part, and the adriatic, which runs more inland and is called the upper sea.
