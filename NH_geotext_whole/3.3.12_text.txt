to the jurisdiction of gades belongs regina, with roman citizens; and laepia, ulia, carisa surnamed aurelia, urgia or castrum julium, likewise called caesaris salutariensis, all of which enjoy latin rights. the tributary towns are besaro, belippo, barbesula, lacippo, baesippo, callet, cappa with oleastro, iptuci, brana, lacibi, saguntia, saudo, usaepo.
