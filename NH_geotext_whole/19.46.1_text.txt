parsley sowing begins at the vernal equinox, the seed being first gently pounded in a mortar: it is thought that the parsley is made crisper by this process, or if the seed is rolled or trodden into the earth after being sown. a peculiarity of parsley is that it changes its colour. achaia it has the distinction of providing the wreath worn by the winners of the sacred contest at neniea.
