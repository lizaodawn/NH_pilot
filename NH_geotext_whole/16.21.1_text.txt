in europe tar is obtained from the torch-pine by heating it, and is used for coating ships' tackle and many other purposes. the wood of the tree is chopped up and put into ovens and heated by means of a fire packed all round outside. the first liquid that exudes flows like water down a pipe; in syria this is called 'cedar-juice,' and it is so strong that in egypt it is used for embalming the bodies of the dead.
