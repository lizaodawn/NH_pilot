the towns in the jurisdiction of hispalis are the following: celti, axati, arua, canama, naeva, ilipa, surnamed ilpa, and italica. on the left of the river is the colony of hispalis named romuliensis, and, on the opposite side, the town of osset, surnamed julia constantia, lucurgentum, or juli genius, orippo caura, siarum, and the river maenuba, which enters the baetis on its right bank. between the aestuaries of the baetis lie the towns of nebrissa, surnamed veneria, and of colobana. the colonies are, hasta, which is also called regia, and, more inland, that of asido, surnamed caesarina.
