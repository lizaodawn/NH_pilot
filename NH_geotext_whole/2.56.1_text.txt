among things that grow in the ground, it does not strike a laurel bush. it never penetrates more than five feet into the earth; consequently when in fear of lightning men think caves of greater depth are the safest, or else a tent made of the skin of the creatures called sea-calves, because that alone among marine animals lightning does not strike, just as it does not strike the eagle among birds; this is why the eagle is represented as armed with a thunderbolt as a weapon. in italy in the time of the caesarian war people ceased to build towers between terracina and the temple of feronia, as every tower there was destroyed by lightning.
