caria. upon the adjoining coast is caria, then ionia, and beyond it aeolis. caria surrounds doris, which lies in the middle, and runs down on both sides of it to the sea. in it is the promontory of pedalium, the river glaucus, into which the telmedium discharges itself, the towns of daedala, crya, peopled by fugitives, the river axon, and the town of calynda.
