magnesia: to thessaly magnesia joins, in which is the fountain of libethra, the towns iolcos, ormenium, pyrrha, methone, olizon, the promontory of sepias, the towns castana and spalathra, the promontory aeantium, the towns meliboea, rhizus, and erymnae (eurymenai); the mouth of the peneus, the towns homolium, orthe, iresiae, pelinna (phalanna), thaumacie, gyrton, crannon, acharne, dotion, melite, phylace, and potniae. the length of epirus, achaia, attica, and thessaly is said altogether to amount to 490 miles, the breadth to 297.
