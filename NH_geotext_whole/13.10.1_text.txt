syria has several trees that are peculiar to it beside this date; in the class of nuts the pistachio is well-known: it is reported that taken either in food or in drink it is a remedy for snakebite. in the fig class syria has the carians and smaller figs of the same class called cottana, also the plum that grows on mount damascus and the myxa, both now acclimated in italy. in egypt the myxa is also used for making wine.
