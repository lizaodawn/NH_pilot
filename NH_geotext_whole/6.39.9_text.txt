thus far we have set forth the results of observations made by the ancients. the remaining part of the earth has been divided, through the careful researches of those of more recent times, by three additional parallels. the first runs from the tanais through the maeotis and the country of the sarmatae, as far as the borysthenes, and so through the daci and part of germany, and the gallic provinces, as far as the shores of the ocean, the longest day being sixteen hours.
