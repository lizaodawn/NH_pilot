in western ethiopia there is a spring, the nigris, which most people have supposed to be the source of the nile, as they try to prove by the arguments that we have stated. in its neighbourhood there is an animal called the catoblepas, in other respects of moderate size and inactive with the rest of its limbs, only with a very heavy head which it carries with difficulty - it is always hanging down to the ground; otherwise it is deadly to the human race, as all who see its eyes expire immediately.
