that portion of these people which inhabit the country adjacent to doris are called phthiotae. their towns are echinus, at the mouth of the river sperchius, and, at four miles from the narrow pass of thermopylae, heraclea, which from it takes its surname of trachin. here too is mount callidromus, and the celebrated towns of hellas, halos, lamia, phthia, and arne.
