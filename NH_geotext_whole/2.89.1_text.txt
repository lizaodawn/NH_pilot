the famous islands of delos and rhodes are recorded in history as having been born from the sea long ago, and subsequently smaller ones, anaphe beyond melos, the neae between lemnos and the dardanelles, halone between lebedos and teos, in the cyclades thera and therasia, and between them, in the 4th year of the 145th olympiad hiera, likewise automate, 130 years later; and 2 stades from hiera, 110 years later, in our age, thia island, on july 8 in the year of the consulship of marcus junius silanus and lucius balbus (19 ad).
