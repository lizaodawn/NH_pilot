nearly the whole of spain abounds in mines of lead, iron, copper, silver, and gold; in the nearer spain there is also found lapis specularis; in baetica there is cinnabar. there are also quarries of marble. the emperor vespasianus augustus, while still harassed by the storms that agitated the roman state, conferred the latin rights on the whole of spain. the pyrenean mountains divide spain from gaul, their extremities projecting into the two seas on either side.
