hyssop crushed in oil is good for phthiriasis and itch on the scalp. the best comes from mount taurus in cilicia, the next best from pamphylia and smyrna. upsetting the stomach, it purges by stool if taken with figs, by vomitings if taken with honey. pounded with honey, salt, and cummin it is also supposed to counteract the poison of snake bites.
