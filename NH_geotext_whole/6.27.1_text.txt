carmania: nearchus states in his writings that the coast of carmania extends a distance of twelve hundred and fifty miles. from its frontier to the river sabis is one hundred miles. at this spot begins the cultivation of the vine; which with the tillage of the fields, extends as far as the river ananis, a distance of twenty-five miles. this region is known by the name of armuzia. the cities of carmania are zetis and alexandria.
