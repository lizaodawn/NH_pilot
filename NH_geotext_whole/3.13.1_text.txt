sardinia: leucothea comes next, and after it, but out of sight, as it lies upon the verge of the african sea, sardinia. it is situate somewhat less than eight miles from the nearest point of corsica, and the straits between them are even still more reduced by the small islands there situate, called the cuniculariae, as also those of phintonis and fossae, from which last the straits themselves have obtained the name of taphros.
