in the vicinity of smyrna are the peristerides, carteria, alopece, elaeussa, bachina, pystira, crommyonnesos, and megale. facing troas there are the ascaniae, and the three islands called plateae. we find also the lamiae, the two islands called plitaniae, plate, scopelos, getone, arthedon, coelae, lagussae, and didymae.
