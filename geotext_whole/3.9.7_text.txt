on the coast we first meet with the river savo, the town of volturnum with a river of the same name, the town of liternum, cumae, a chalcidian colony, misenum, the port of baiae, bauli, the lucrine lake, and lake avernus, near which there stood formerly a town of the cimmerians. we then come to puteoli, formerly called the colony of dicaearchia, then the phlegraean plains, and the marsh of acherusia in the vicinity of cumae.
