the two mauritanias: the greeks have given the name of libya to africa, and have called the sea that lies in front of it the libyan sea. it has egypt for its boundary, and no part of the earth is there that has fewer gulfs or inlets, its shores extending in a lengthened line from the west in an oblique direction. the names of its peoples, and its cities in especial, cannot possibly be pronounced with correctness, except by the aid of their own native tongues. its population, too, for the most part dwells only in fortresses.
